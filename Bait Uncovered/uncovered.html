Bait Uncovered

"This green helps lose 10lbs of fat weekly!" (the article only talks about drinking green tea without proving the claim.)
Headlines like "SCIENTISTS PROVE COFFEE CURES EVERY ILLNESS" (instead of explaining a nuanced trial.)
Phrases like "experts say" "officials claim" or "many doctors reveal" without naming them.
Ex: An article covering a political debate that only lists quotes from one candidate.
Ex: Using an image from a 2018 music festival to claim it represents a breaking news riot happening today.
The image has weirdly shaped extra fingers, warped text in the background, or an overly glossy cartoon sheen.
